Good Questions

Teams & Spiritwear, Answered.

More on the full FAQ page, or see how to order custom shirts for the start-to-finish process.

Do you print uniforms for youth and rec sports teams?
Yes. We print for youth leagues, travel teams, rec programs, high school clubs, and adult leagues across DuPage County. Jerseys, practice tees, warmups, hoodies, and coach gear, from a handful of pieces to a full league order.
Can you add names and numbers to uniforms?
Yes. Player names and numbers are one of our most common requests. We handle personalization with DTF transfers pressed in-house, so every jersey in the run gets the right name, the right number, and the same durable finish.
Is there a minimum order for team uniforms or spiritwear?
No. Order as few as one piece. Per-piece pricing improves with quantity, and screens are free on orders of 100 or more pieces and on all reorders, which most seasonal spiritwear orders are.
How does a school spirit wear store work?
We build your school or team a free online store with your designs on it. Families order and pay online, pick their own sizes, and we print, pack, and ship. No spreadsheets, no collecting cash, no chasing down a parent for a youth medium.
How far ahead should we order for a season?
Standard turnaround is 10 to 12 business days plus shipping, and local teams can pick up in Warrenville to skip shipping entirely. For a season kickoff or a big school event, get your quote in 3 to 4 weeks out so there's room for proofs, sizing, and roster changes. Rush is available by quote.
