Why Choose a Local Screen Printing Shop Instead of an Online Service?

By Matt Marcotte · January 25, 2023 · Screen Printing

When it comes to screen printing, a lot of people default to online services for the convenience and the low sticker price. But there are real reasons a local screen printing shop is the better call.

Personalized Service

The staff at a local shop are experts in screen printing and can guide you on design, materials, and printing techniques. That hands-on advice, built from years of experience, is especially valuable if you're new to ordering custom apparel.

See and Approve a Sample First

A local shop lets you see and approve a proof before the final product runs. That means better quality control and a finished product that actually matches what you pictured. Online services often don't offer the same level of communication, which is how people end up disappointed with what shows up.

Faster Turnaround

Because the printing happens on-site, local shops can often complete orders faster and respond to rush or emergency jobs in a timely way.

Support the Local Economy

Money spent at a local screen printer stays in the community, helping create and sustain jobs. It's a small choice that adds up for your neighbors.

A More Sustainable Approach

Local shops tend to lean on more eco-friendly methods and materials, with a more sustainable approach to the printing process overall.

More Customization Options

Special ink colors, different fabrics, and unique designs are all on the table at a local shop, so you can create products that truly represent your brand or message.

Bottom line: a local screen printing shop offers personalized service, quality control, quick turnaround, community support, an eco-friendly approach, and more ways to customize. Next time you need printing done, consider going local for the best results. If you're ready, start a quote and a real person will get back to you fast.
