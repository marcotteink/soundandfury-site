Good Questions

Answers Before You Ask.

The stuff people ask us most. There's more on the full FAQ page.

How fast can you print my order?
Standard turnaround is 10 to 12 business days plus shipping. Rush is available by custom quote. Orders placed after 5:00 PM process the next business day.
What's the minimum order?
There's no minimum. Order as few as one piece. Smaller runs print on our Premium Film Print process (digital screen print / DTF) with no screen fees, and you can mix sizes, garment styles, and shirt colors in one order as long as the ink colors stay the same.
Can you build an online store for my team or business?
Yes. We set up a free online merch store for teams, schools, companies, and brands. Everyone orders their own size and pays online; we print, pack, and ship.
Do you ever waive screen and setup fees?
Screens are free on orders of 100+ pieces per design, and screens are always free on reorders.
