The 5 Most Effective Promotional Products for the Chicago Area

By Tiffany D. Davidson · September 19, 2018 · Updated July 1, 2026 · Promo

The average American produces nearly five pounds of garbage a day. So before you order a pile of promotional items, ask the hard question: is this something people will actually keep, or is it headed straight for the trash? Branded fidget spinners came and went. Stress balls live in a drawer. The promo products that survive are the ones that line up with your brand and earn a spot in someone's daily life.

Think about it from the recipient's side. A branded pen from a screen-printing shop doesn't say much. A well-designed sticker, on the other hand, has real aesthetic value and gets people curious enough to look you up. Here are the five that consistently pull their weight.

1. Apparel

Simple, comfortable, well-branded clothing turns your customers and team into walking advertisements. Few promo items get as many impressions as a shirt someone actually wants to wear, and unlike a social ad, nobody scrolls past a person. The same logic powers team uniforms and spiritwear: gear people are proud to wear does the marketing for you.

2. Stickers

Affordable and endlessly creative, and somehow more relevant than ever: laptops, water bottles, hard hats, and car windows are all free billboard space. Stickers also open the door to fun campaigns, like offering a discount when customers photograph your sticker out in the wild and tag you online.

3. Business Cards

In a world of QR codes and "I'll just text you," a physical card on heavy stock with a clean design stands out more than it did a decade ago, not less. A great card signals that you take your work seriously, and people notice. Add a QR code to your quote page and the card does double duty.

4. Banners & Signage

Essential for events. Banners and signage create the first impression and pull people toward your booth, table, or storefront.

5. Window Clings & Decals

A cost-effective alternative to a billboard. Rotate the promotional content as your offers change, and let perforated vinyl do the advertising from your window.
