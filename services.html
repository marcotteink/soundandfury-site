What We Do

Everything Your Brand Wears, Carries & Hangs Up.

Apparel is the heart of the shop, but we print, stitch, and build the whole system around it. Here's how we can help, start to finish, all from one shop in Warrenville, IL.

01 Screen Printing

Screen-Printed Apparel

The main event. Tees, hoodies, crewnecks, and team gear printed in-house on our automatic and manual presses, with oversized prints up to 15" x 22". We dial in the ink and the cure so color stays loud wash after wash.

  • ⚡ No order minimums, order as few as you need
  • ⚡ Free screens on orders of 100+ pieces and on all reorders
  • ⚡ Mix sizes, styles, and shirt colors in one run (same ink colors)
  • ⚡ Best for: bulk tees, team gear, events, band & brand merch

02 Embroidery

Embroidery

Hats, polos, button-downs, jackets, and bags with a stitched, premium feel. Embroidery is the right call when your brand needs to look as good in the boardroom as it does on stage, or when you want a finish that lasts for years.

  • ⚡ Headwear, corporate apparel, uniforms, bags
  • ⚡ Clean digitizing so your logo stitches sharp
  • ⚡ Pairs perfectly with screen-printed tees for a full kit
  • ⚡ Best for: company wear, premium hats, polished branding

03 DTF & Small Runs

DTF & Premium Film Print

Need fewer than 24 pieces, photorealistic color, or wild gradients? Our Premium Film Print process (digital screen print / DTF) delivers vibrant, durable prints with no screen fees. Small orders, zero compromise.

  • ⚡ No screen fees, ideal for runs under 24 pieces
  • ⚡ Full-color, photo-real artwork and gradients
  • ⚡ Pressed in-house on our heat presses
  • ⚡ Best for: small batches, complex art, names & numbers
