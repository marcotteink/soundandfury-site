The Ultimate Guide to the Best Custom Logo Hats

By Matt Marcotte · June 29, 2023 · Embroidery

Custom hats pull double duty. They provide sun protection, add style, and hide a bad hair day, but more importantly they're a branding tool, putting your logo or your event front and center wherever the wearer goes. This guide runs through our favorite blank hats for embroidery, and who each one is right for.

The Crowd-Pleaser: Richardson 112

The Richardson 112 Snapback Trucker is the bestselling trucker for a reason. It uses a 60/40 cotton/polyester fabric that gives an ideal surface for all types and sizes of embroidery. The snapback closure makes it one-size-friendly, and it comes in over 30 colors, so it's hard to go wrong.

The Fitted Trucker With RFlex

The Richardson Fitted Trucker with RFlex blends features from several hat styles. Its Comfort Stretch fabric, a mix of polyester, cotton, and spandex, gives you durability and a flexible fit that works indoors and out.

Port Authority Snapback Trucker

A reliable choice with 100% cotton twill front panels that take just about any embroidered logo or name, plus a polyester mesh back for breathability and a snap closure.

Port Authority Six-Panel Unstructured Twill

An affordable, stylish option in an 80/20 polyester/cotton blend that adds water resistance and sun protection. The one-size-fits-all design keeps bulk ordering simple.

New Era Flat Bill Snapback

Modern and unisex. This high-profile 100% cotton cap stays breathable and holds its color and shape wash after wash.

Port & Company Knit Cap

For winter, this 100% acrylic beanie has a three-inch folding cuff, a wide surface that's perfect for an embroidered logo.

Port Authority Bucket Hat

A unisex, versatile pick in 100% cotton twill with a wide brim for solid face and neck coverage.

Port Authority Adjustable Mesh Back

A mid-profile cap with 100% cotton twill front panels, polyester mesh backing, and a hook-and-loop closure. A clean fit for corporate settings.

47 Brand Clean-Up Cap

Simple and sleek, made from 100% garment-washed cotton twill with an unstructured low profile, pre-curved visor, and a snap-slide buckle.

Sport-Tek Performance Knit Hat

A minimalist cotton/acrylic beanie in five colors. Warm, clean, and easy to brand.

Flexfit 110 Comfort Fit Visor

Perfect for the folks with a lot of hair. This open-top visor uses resilient polyester/spandex fabric with Permacurv visor technology for sun protection.
