Good Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

Minimums, turnaround, art files, and the stuff that trips people up. If your question isn't here, call 630.791.8099.

How fast can you print my order?
Standard turnaround is 10 to 12 business days plus shipping. Rush is available by custom quote via email. Orders placed after 5:00 PM process the next business day.
What's the minimum order?
There's no minimum order. You can order as few as one piece. Smaller runs print on the Premium Film Print process (digital screen print / DTF) with no screen fees, and you can mix sizes, garment styles, and shirt colors in one order as long as the ink colors stay the same.
Can you build an online store for my team or business?
Yes. We set up a free online merch store for teams, schools, companies, and brands. Everyone orders their own size and pays online; we print, pack, and ship. More on online stores.
What art files do you accept?
PSD, EPS, PDF, or AI at 300 dpi or higher. Files under 10MB can be emailed; send bigger files via a Dropbox link. Convert Illustrator text to outlines. No art? Use our Design Lab.
Can I see blank apparel options before I order?
Yes. Browse blanks.soundandfuryprint.com for live inventory, brands, and colors. Custom sourcing is available by quote.
Why does white ink on a black shirt count as two colors?
Dark garments need an underbase, a white layer printed first so the top colors stay bright, like primer before paint. Skipping it gives a softer "vintage" look but sacrifices vibrancy.
Do you ever waive screen and setup fees?
Screens are free on orders of 100+ pieces per design, and screens are always free on reorders.
