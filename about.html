We're Sound & Fury.

An independent screen printing shop, creative house, and lifestyle brand in the Chicago suburbs. We've been printing for over a decade, and we're just getting louder.

The Story

Together, We Create.

It's painted on our wall and it's printed into everything we do. Sound & Fury started as a small shop with big opinions about what good printing should look like, and grew into a full creative house serving the Chicago area and shipping worldwide.

Over those years we've printed for first-time bands ordering their very first run of merch, all the way up to Fortune 500 corporate and event apparel. The clients that make an impact, big or small, tend to share the same traits: a clear idea, good art, and a printer who actually cares how it comes out.

That printer is us. We treat a single-shirt order with the same attention as a 1,200-piece one. No minimums, ever.

Proudly Local

Printing for Chicagoland.

We're rooted in Warrenville and we love working with our neighbors. Choosing a local shop means personalized service, real proofs, faster turnarounds, and money that stays in the community.
