What to Include

Help Us Quote It Right the First Time.

Every quote request goes to a real person at the shop, not an algorithm. We review your job, price it out per piece, and send it back fast. The more of this you can tell us, the tighter your first quote will be:

  • ⚡ The garment. Brand and style if you know it, or just describe the feel and budget you want. You can browse everything in our blank catalog.
  • ⚡ Quantity and sizes. A rough count is fine to start. There's no minimum, and orders of 100+ pieces per design get free screens.
  • ⚡ Ink colors and print locations. Front, back, sleeve, tag. Fewer ink colors usually means a lower per-piece price on screen printing.
  • ⚡ Your artwork. Vector files print cleanest, but send what you have. No art yet? Start in the Design Lab and our artists will help.
  • ⚡ Your deadline. Standard turnaround is 10 to 12 business days plus shipping. Rush is available by quote.

Not sure whether your job should be screen printed, embroidered, or DTF? Our guide to screen printing vs. DTF vs. embroidery breaks it down, or just tell us in the notes and we'll recommend the right method for your art, garment, and budget.
